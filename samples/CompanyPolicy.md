# Company Policy & Employee Handbook

## 1. Work Hours & Attendance
Our standard working hours are **9:00 AM to 5:00 PM**, Monday through Friday.
- **Flexible Hours**: Employees may start between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM.
- **Remote Work**: We follow a hybrid model. Employees are expected to be in the office on Tuesdays and Thursdays.

## 2. Leave Policy
- **Sick Leave**: 10 days per year.
- **Casual Leave**: 12 days per year.
- **Privilege Leave**: 15 days per year (encashable).

## 3. IT & Security
- **Passwords**: Must be changed every 90 days.
- **Devices**: Only company-issued laptops are allowed on the internal network.
- **VPN**: Use the company VPN when accessing resources remotely.

## 4. Tools & Software
- **Slack**: Primary communication tool.
- **Jira**: Project management and issue tracking.
- **Zoom**: Video conferencing (use the corporate account).

## 5. Expense Reimbursement
- **Travel**: All business travel must be pre-approved.
- **Meals**: Up to $50 per day during business travel.
- **Submission**: Submit receipts via the HR portal by the 25th of each month.
